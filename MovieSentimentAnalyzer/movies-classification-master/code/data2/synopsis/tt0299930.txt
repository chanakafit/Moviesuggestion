Larry Gigli (Ben Affleck) is a small-time hood in Los Angeles, who gets a mob assignment to kidnap Brian (Justin Bartha), the psychologically challenged younger brother of a federal prosecutor who is trying to send a New York mob boss, Starkman (Al Pacino) to prison. Gigli's boss, Louis (Lenny Venito) doesn't trust Gigli with this important job, so he sends in reinforcements in the form of a beautiful lesbian assassin, Ricki (Jennifer Lopez). Gigli tries to win the affections of Ricki until he finds out that she is a lesbian. Douglas Young (the-movie-guy)
