The movie is a romantic comedy about a young man, Tom Stansfield played by Ashton Kutcher, who is asked to housesit his boss's mansion and take care of his owl while the boss is out of town for one night. Tom thinks this is a perfect opportunity for him to get close to Lisa Taylor the boss's daughter played by Tara Reid. During that one long night, Tom must deal with Lisa's older brother played by Andy Richter who has some dealings with the local drug dealer. There is also a long line of other unexpected houseguests who take over and mess up the house. With all these crazy people in the house, Tom thinks he will lose his job and the girl when the boss comes home. Douglas Young (the-movie-guy)
