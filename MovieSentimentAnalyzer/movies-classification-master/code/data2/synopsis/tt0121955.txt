This adult animated show follows the exploits of for boys in the quiet mountain town of South Park, Colorado. Stan, Kyle, Kenny, and Eric fight the forces of evil, including genetically engineered towels, crab people, man/bear/pig hybrids, TV execs, and each other.
The four boys face the challenges of growing up with crazy parents, crazy friends, and crazy people. This Emmy winning series explores topics like homosexuality, death, the media, and education in every episode.
