New adventures of Doctor Who, Millionaire scientist whom just happened to unlock the secrets of time and space travel while darning his socks. The series is surrounded in mystery and is action orientated from the very first episode. A gang of bank robbers terrorize a small town by knocking on doors and then killing whoever answers, It could be your street... your house... your life, join in each week as Dr, Robert Who finds more clues leading to the arrest, and maybe conviction, of the robbers!
